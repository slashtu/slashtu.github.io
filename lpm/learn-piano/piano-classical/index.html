Course Overview

What you will learn

The Classical Piano core program for beginners teaches you how to play the piano from the ground up, including

  • correct posture and hand positions
  • fundamental music theory topics such as key signature, meter, basic note values and rhythms, and musical terms
  • five-finger patterns
  • scales and arpeggios
  • triads and chord progressions

You will be guided step by step to learn these essential piano skills while working on interesting music examples and repertoire. The music theory knowledge you need for this program is also integrated in the lessons. If you have never played the piano or want to freshen up the basic techniques, come join us!
